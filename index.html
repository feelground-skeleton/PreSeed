What FeelGround Is

A mental wellness ecosystem combining structured programs, trained human facilitators, and plant-based supplement support — in one integrated platform that adapts as users evolve. The system does the work. Users don't have to.

We sit in the whitespace between clinical treatment and abandonment — serving the majority who can't access or don't want psychiatry, but need more than an app. Structured. Evidence-measured. Human-led.

FeelGround also solves a supply-side gap — connecting gifted facilitators, coaches, and healers who have walked the journey themselves to a wider audience and sustainable income. People who speak directly to who their clients are, in ways traditional models never could. FeelGround gives them the platform, the structure, and the reach that would otherwise take years and significant resources to build independently.

Built for the Global South first — South Africa, Africa, India — markets that have been left behind by global wellness platforms. Long-term vision: the defining mental wellness operating system for the majority of the world that existing platforms have never truly reached.
